Biography

The band was founded in 1997 in Ivano-Frankivsk. Initially, the band members positioned the style as “Ukrainian alternative metal”. Over time, they began to use their own definition of "hutsul-metal" [1] . The band released their first album in 2003 under the name Leto . The second album was released in 2010 under the name "KARNA" and was included in the top 100 albums of the Ukrainian Independence, according to the music edition Karabas Live [2] . In 2017, a third album was released entitled "Hutsul Metal" [3] . In the period between the release of the albums, the band actively appeared both on the local stage (in particular, initiating the first Ivano-Frankivsk rock-festival "Rock Explosion" [4] ), as well as on the scale of Ukraine and beyond. Among the awards of the team are the diploma finalists of the Chervona Ruta Festival 2001 and the Grand Prix of the Taras Bulba Festival 2003.
